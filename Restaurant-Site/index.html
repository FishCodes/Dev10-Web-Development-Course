Since it was opened in 1942, Kenny's American Dream Restaurant has been a staple of downtown Manassas. Growing up on a small farm outside the town, Kenny Miller would take some of the crops his parents harvested and experiment with his own recipes. Eventually, Kenny started his own restaurant in order to share his food with the community. When one of his first customers took their first bite, they said, "Now this is the American Dream!" The name stuck, and Kenny's American Dream Restaurant became known across the state.

To this day, Kenny's only uses ingredients grown locally, ensuring that each and every mouthful is filled with the freshest ingredients. From burgers to salads, come get a taste of the American Dream at Kenny's American Dream Restaurant!
